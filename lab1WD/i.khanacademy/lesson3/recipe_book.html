Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 500g
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Onion 1, chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté the chopped onion and garlic until soft.

Step 3: Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned.

Step 4: Stir in the tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 5: Serve the bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Enjoy!

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Cocoa powder 50g
Sugar 200g
Baking powder 1 tsp
Eggs 2
Milk 150ml
Butter 100g

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a cake pan.

Step 2: In a bowl, mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.

Step 3: Add eggs, milk, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.

Step 4: Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step 5: Let the cake cool before serving. Optionally, frost with chocolate icing.
